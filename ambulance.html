Royal Nursing Home (Pvt)Ltd. has the most number of ambulances owned by a private entity and all our ambulances are equipped with the latest technology. These ambulances have ICU facilities equipped with the cutting-edge life-saving equipment including defibrillators and mobile ventilators and our ambulance beds use shockproof Air Suspension technology that prevents any shaking and tossing, which in turn makes the journey to the hospital less stressful for the patient. We place great emphasis on the safety of the patient during transport and we are the only company that provides facilities that are compatible with international standards at a reasonable price.
