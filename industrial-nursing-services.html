INDUSTRIAL NURSING SERVICES



We will establish, at Board of Investment (BOI) approved factories, a 24 hour medical center and an ambulance service through our industrial nursing services. Our doctors and nurses will be present at the centers throughout the day and are capable of handling any emergency.

This is particularly important in the industrial landscape since on-site healthcare protection is one of the prerequisites for receiving a BOI accreditation & ISO Standard.

Royal Nursing Home(Pvt)Ltd. are geared to boost your business and industrial performances and then profits with various ways and means. Today's competitive business environment, human resources are the most important assets. When you are our service user for Industrial Nursing Care, it will not only fulfill the quality andstandard requirement of your business to be competitive in the world, but also it will help you to enhance your employee satisfaction drastically.


The following are the exclusiveness of our Industrial Nursing Service


* Dispense first aid treatment in an emergency.
* Accompany patients with safe transportation to hospitals as and when necessary.
* Attend to minor ailments like headaches, stomach aches, fainting attacks etc.
* Observe and record temperature, pulse, respiration and blood pressure.
* Surgical dressing of wounds/Administering of intra-muscular injections.
* Doctor on call facility.
* As per the requirement First Aid training will be provided to the entire staff.
* Training on safety, Care and Employee performance enhancement.
