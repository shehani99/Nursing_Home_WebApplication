COVID-19 CARING SERVICES

We are ready to serve your loving parents who are being treated for COVID-19 at home or in a hospital.

Facilities and privileges

  1. 24 Hours caregiver fixed concessionary fee for 10 days
  2. Complete COVID-19 protective clothing and accessories will be providing by the institution to the attending caregiver in accordance with WHO guidelines.
  3. 24-Hour hospitalization ambulance service for emergencies at discounted rates as required.
  4. Doctor video consulttation
  5. Twice a day remote monitorining of vitals by nurse
  6. Ten days after the end of the service of the caregiver, they will be quarantined, and the expences will be borne by the institution as well as the necessary treatement needed if the nurse is diagnosed with COVID-19. The client is not responsible for those activities.
  7. Oxygen Cylinder is available at a discounted rate as required.
